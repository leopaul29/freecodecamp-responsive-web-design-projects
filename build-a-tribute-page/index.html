Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs, co-founder and former CEO of Apple Inc.

Here's a time line of Steve Jobs's life

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco
  • 1968 - Job internship at Hewlett Packard
  • 1970 - Meets Wozniak
  • 1972 - Graduiates from Hemestead High School and enrolls at Reed College
  • 1973 - Briefly works at Atari
  • 1976 - Co-founds Apple Computer and begin selling the Apple I
  • 1977 - Apple Incoporates and release of the Apple II
  • 1984 - Macintosh
  • 1985 - Leaves Apple and starts NeXT Computer
  • 1986 - Purchases Graphics Division of Lucasfilm that becomes Pixar
  • 1988 - First Generation NeXT Computer is released
  • 1997 - Becomes CEO of Apple
  • 1998 - Introduces the iMac
  • 2001 - Introduces the IPod
  • 2004 - Introduces the IPod Shuffle
  • 2005 - Introduces the IPod Nano
  • 2007 - Introduces the IPhone
  • 2010 - Introduces the IPad
  • 2011 - Passes away
